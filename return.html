Refund and Returns Policy

Stay informed with the latest updates, policies, and insights from The Book Lounge

Return Eligibility

  • Books must be returned within 14 days of purchase
  • Books must be in their original condition (unread, no markings, no damage)
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase is required
  • Books must include all original packaging and materials
  • Digital or e-books cannot be returned

Return Process

  1. Contact us at info@thebooklounge.com with your order details
  2. Provide a clear reason for the return
  3. We will provide you with return instructions
  4. Ship the book back to us (return shipping is the customer's responsibility)
  5. Once we receive and inspect the book, we will process your refund

Refunds

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunded amount will be the full purchase price minus any applicable shipping fees
  • Refunds do not include return shipping costs

Damaged or Defective Books

If you receive a damaged or defective book, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Exceptions

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Books marked as final sale
  • Used or read books
  • Books with water damage or stains
  • Items purchased during clearance sales

Questions about our policy? Feel free to contact us, and we'll be happy to help!
