Privacy Policy

Who We Are

The Book Lounge is an independent bookstore located in Cape Town's CBD. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent relationship with our customers and website visitors.

Website: The Book Lounge | Contact: 71 Roeland Street, Cape Town

Data We Collect & Why

Comments & Communications

When you leave comments on our site, we collect your submitted data, IP address, and browser information to help detect spam. An anonymized version of your email address may be used with Gravatar to display your profile picture with comments.

Media & Uploads

If you upload images to our website, please avoid including embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Visitors can extract location information from images, so be mindful of your privacy when uploading.

Contact Forms

Information submitted through our contact forms is used solely to respond to your inquiries and improve our services.

Cookies & Tracking

  • Comment Cookies: Saves your name, email, and website for up to 1 year for convenience
  • Login Cookies: Temporary cookies to verify browser acceptance; login cookies last 2 days
  • Session Cookies: "Remember Me" option persists login for 2 weeks
  • Post Cookies: Tracks edited posts for 1 day; contains no personal data

All cookies are cleared when you log out or close your browser.

Embedded Content

Our website may include embedded content from external sources (videos, images, articles). These embedded elements may collect data about you, use their own cookies, and track your interactions independently of our site.

Data Retention

Comments

Comments and metadata are retained indefinitely to recognize and auto-approve follow-up comments.

User Accounts

Personal information in registered accounts is stored in user profiles. Users can view, edit, or delete their information anytime (except usernames). Website administrators can also manage this data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request an exported file of all personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data (excluding legally required records)
  • Access, edit, or delete your account information at any time

Data Sharing

Your data is shared only when necessary for service delivery. Visitor comments are checked through automated spam detection services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Protection

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Sensitive data is encrypted and securely stored
  • Access is limited to authorized personnel only
  • In case of data breaches, affected users will be notified promptly
